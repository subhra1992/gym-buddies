How to Perform Incline Bench Press

  1. Set an incline bench to a 30–45° angle.
  2. Lie back on the bench with a dumbbell in each hand resting on your thighs.
  3. Kick the dumbbells up one at a time to shoulder level as you lie back.
  4. Press the weights up above your chest by extending your arms.
  5. Lower the dumbbells slowly in a controlled motion to chest level.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of reps, usually 8–12.

Targeted Muscles

Primarily targets the upper chest (clavicular head of the pectoralis major), front deltoids, and triceps.

Tips

  • Don't flare your elbows too wide — keep them at about a 45° angle.
  • Use a full range of motion for maximum muscle activation.
  • Control the tempo — avoid bouncing the weights.
