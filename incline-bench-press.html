Incline Dumbbell Bench Press

Muscles Worked: Upper chest, anterior deltoids, triceps.

Instructions:

  • Set a bench to a 30–45° incline.
  • Grab two dumbbells and lie back on the bench.
  • Press the dumbbells upward with a slight inward angle.
  • Lower slowly, keeping control and elbows at ~45°.
  • Repeat for 8–12 reps, maintaining form.
